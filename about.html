About Me

Meet Kristine Kahler, Founder of Spatialytics Solutions

My Story

I’m Kristine Kahler, a GIS professional with over 10 years of experience in geospatial technology and IT solutions. With a Master’s in Geospatial Technology, I specialize in ArcGIS, QGIS, Python, SQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. As the founder of Spatialytics Solutions, I craft custom web applications and data-driven innovations that transform complex data into actionable insights.

My passion for geospatial storytelling shines through projects like Echoes of the Land, an interactive map of Indigenous trails, and a Minnesota storm chasing website with real-time weather integration. I’ve also developed travel booking platforms (e.g., Yeti Travel Agency with Disney and Virgin Voyages APIs) and automated tools like the Wind Turbine Trip Planner. With a background at HDR Inc. and National Grid, I bring corporate expertise to freelance creativity.

Based in Minnesota, I draw inspiration from the region’s landscapes—especially in this fall season—to create user-friendly, impactful solutions for utilities, nonprofits, and businesses.
